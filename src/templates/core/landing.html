Secure & Private

Token-based voting ensures only eligible, verified students can participate — one vote per person per level. Your ballot is completely anonymous.

Transparent Results

Live vote counting with real-time results. No back-room counting — results are published the moment polls close.

Multi-Level Elections

Supports President, State Leader, and Course Representative elections. Each voter automatically gets tokens for their eligible positions.

Works Everywhere

Fully responsive — vote from your phone, tablet, or laptop. No app install needed.

How to Vote
  1. Create your account
    Use your university registration number to verify and register.
  2. Wait for verification
    The Election Commissioner verifies your account and issues voting tokens.
  3. Review candidates
    Read each candidate's bio and platform before deciding.
  4. Cast your vote
    Submit during the active window — votes are final and anonymous.
  5. Check results
    Live results publish as soon as the election closes.
About MWECAUSO
Mwenge Catholic University Student Government Organization

MWECAUSO is the official student government of Mwenge Catholic University, representing students across all faculties, courses, and states on campus.

This platform modernizes student elections — replacing paper ballots with a digital system that is faster, fairer, and fully auditable.
