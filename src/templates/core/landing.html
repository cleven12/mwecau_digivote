Secure & Private

Every vote is anonymous. Token-based voting ensures only eligible, verified students can participate — one vote per person, per level.

Transparent Results

Live vote counting with real-time results visible to all. No back-room counting, no surprises — just democracy in action.

Multi-Level Elections

Support for President, State Leader, and Course Representative elections — each voter gets the right tokens for their eligible positions.

Works Everywhere

Fully responsive — vote from your phone, tablet, or laptop. No app needed, just your registration number and password.

How to Vote
  1. Create your account
    Use your university registration number to verify and register.
  2. Wait for verification
    The Election Commissioner will verify your account and issue voting tokens.
  3. Review candidates
    Read each candidate's bio and platform before deciding.
  4. Cast your vote
    Submit your vote during the active election window. Votes are final.
  5. Check results
    Live results are published as soon as the election closes.
About MWECAUSO
Mwenge Catholic University Student Government Organization

MWECAUSO is the official student government of Mwenge Catholic University, representing students across all faculties, courses, and states on campus.

This platform was built to modernize student elections — replacing paper ballots with a digital system that is faster, fairer, and fully auditable.

For support, contact the ICT Office: admin@mwecau.ac.tz
